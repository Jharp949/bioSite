About

Profile

Tabitha has an affinity for the arts and a matching flair for performance. Her favorite hobbies are painting, ceramics, and music. Her favorite art era is Baroque. When she is listening to music, she chooses a playlist to match her mood. One of her go to music styles is Jazz. During leisure time Tabitha enjoys socializing with friends and enjoying a drink during a night out on the town. One of her favorite places to visit is the Huntington Library. She enjoys perusing the art collections and becoming lost in the gardens that the library has to offer. Her dream vacation spot is Switzerland. She has not visited there yet and would relish the chance to take in the art and architecture the country has to offer. Her favorite pet animal is a cat. Anyone who knows her will have their cell phone flooded with cat memes. Her favorite color is split between two depending on the application. If it is light, her favorite color is rose. If it is generic, then her favorite color is dark blue. Her favorite movie is Mean Girls, and her favorite show is Hadestown. When asked what type of animal she would be, she chose jellyfish.

Work Experience

She currently works as a Stage Technician with a specialty in lighting. She held a position with similar responsibilities while living in New York and working at CNN and Juilliard art school. Her goal is to hold a similar title with the current company she works for
